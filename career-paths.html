Explore Career Paths After 10th Class

Choose the right path for your future. Explore different streams and career opportunities available after 10th grade.

Science

The Science stream offers opportunities in engineering, medicine, technology, and research. It's ideal for those who love mathematics, physics, or biology.

Commerce

Commerce opens up careers in business, finance, accounting, and entrepreneurship. Perfect for those interested in economics and management.

Arts/Humanities

Arts offer careers in literature, history, psychology, sociology, journalism, and more. Best suited for creative and socially inclined individuals.

Vocational Courses

Vocational courses provide practical skills for careers in fields like hospitality, IT, healthcare, and design.

Polytechnic Diploma

Get hands-on technical education and pursue careers in engineering, manufacturing, and applied sciences.

Industrial Training Institute (ITI)

Ideal for students interested in practical trades like electrician, mechanic, plumber, and more.

Creative Arts

Explore careers in fashion design, graphic design, photography, and fine arts. Perfect for creative thinkers.

Sports

Pursue a career in athletics, coaching, or sports management. Ideal for students passionate about physical fitness and teamwork.
